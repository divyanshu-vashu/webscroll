FAQs

Cyber Resilience involves safeguarding information, ensuring recoverability, availability, and complying with regulations. It includes measures like backup and restore, data immutability, and long-term retention, functions.
Cyber resilience is crucial because it:

Ensures Data Availability: Allows information that has been protected to be recovered in the event of any data loss including system failure, human error, corruption or a cyber-attack.

Preserves Fundamental Rights: Laws like the EU GDPR recognise cyber resilience as a right.

Builds Trust: Effective protection fosters confidence with customers and partners.

Enhances Brand Reputation: Demonstrating commitment to data protection improves an organisation’s image.

Provides Cyber Resilience: Ensures critical data can be recovered in the event of a cyber-attack to resume business operations.

Saves Time and Costs: Proper protection minimises downtime and resource loss.
Dell offers comprehensive cyber resilience solutions to safeguard critical information across various environments. Solution categories include:

Backup and Recovery: Dell provides proven backup and recovery software and hardware tools to protect business-critical data. These solutions ensure data availability and mitigate the risk of loss.

Data Archiving: Dell’s cyber resilience solutions include archiving capabilities, allowing organisations to manage data efficiently and meet regulatory requirements.

Cloud Backup Software: Dell helps protect data in and across cloud environments, ensuring secure backups and seamless and flexible recovery.

Cyber Recovery: Dell Technologies delivers cyber recovery solutions to preserve data integrity and confidentiality, and isolate critical data from threats. These solutions use AI-based machine learning and analytics to identify suspicious activity and accelerate data recovery.

Dell cyber resilience solutions include PowerProtect Data Domain, PowerProtect Data Manager and PowerProtect Cyber Recovery.

In summary, Dells cyber resilience offerings secure important information, simply management and reduce costs.
