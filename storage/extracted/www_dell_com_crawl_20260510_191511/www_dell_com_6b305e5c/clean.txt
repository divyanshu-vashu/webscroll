The Dell Advantage
Modular by Design. Open, Flexible, Future-proof.
Simplifying Data Storage
Store and manage structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data with Dell Storage Engines. Unified File and Object storage supports hybrid environments, while S3 Object Storage ensures cloud-native scalability.
Curating Data for Al
Prepare and manage data from apps, devices, and other sources with Dell Data Engines. They clean, organize, and optimize information for analytics, Al training, and real-time decision-making.
Securing Data Confidently
Protect sensitive information with built-in security, including access controls, data masking, and encryption. Cyber-resilient features like data isolation safeguard data during potential attacks.
Automate Al Data Pipelines
Connect and orchestrate pipelines, models, and humans across the full Al lifecycle with Dell Orchestration Engine. Support building and deploying workflows for unstructured, multimodal data across cloud, on-prem, and hybrid environments.
