Industry trends shaping energy’s future
Growing global demand
Global energy demand is projected to grow significantly, driven in part by the rapid expansion of AI and data centers, which are creating localized spikes in energy consumption.
Shifting energy mix
By 2035, renewables are expected to generate 60% of the world’s energy, challenging existing grid models that are not equipped to handle 60% intermittent generating capacity.
Grid modernization
The U.S. alone will require a staggering $2 trillion grid modernization investment by 2030 to ensure grid resilience and reliability driven by the need for more energy and the shift in the energy mix.
Energy sustainability
Large investments in environmental performance technology is needed to automate compliance as demand growth is pressurizing existing sustainability plans.
